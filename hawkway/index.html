上海霍卫工艺设备有限公司

SHANGHAI HAWKWAY PROCESS SOLUTIONS & EQUIPMENT CO.,LTD.

上海霍卫工艺设备有限公司及其子公司江苏霍卫机械设备有限公司，是集精馏/薄膜蒸发/结晶/混合与反应/萃取工艺开发及设备生产供应商；目前在上海闵行区设有工艺设计及研发中心，江苏启东市设有生产基地及测试中心，其生产的板波纹填料、丝网填料、塔盘、结晶器、萃取塔及聚合物脱挥等分离设备均达到国际领先水平。

霍卫提供精馏/薄膜蒸发/结晶/混合与反应/萃取工艺开发、精馏塔设计及结晶器设计、聚合物脱挥工艺开发及设计、模块化设计及制造等整体服务，服务覆盖石化，油脂化工，特殊化学品，精细化工，食品，制药和聚合物等行业领域。尤其在精馏结晶萃取联用分离同分异构体的工艺开发和设计，模块化设计和制造，以及聚合物脱挥和反应等方面有着独特的工程经验。

霍卫拥有经验丰富的专家团队，拥有超过15年以上工艺工程设计和生产制造经验；自公司成立以来，霍卫已陆续为行业客户提供近千台塔器和上百套解决方案，累计年销售额上亿元。

Shanghai Hawkway Process Solutions & Equipment Co., Ltd. along with its wholly owned subsidiary, Jiangsu Hawkway Machinery Equipment Co., Ltd. specializes in the development of chemical processes, which involve one or a combination of the following units such as distillation, evaporation, crystallization, mixing, polymerization and extraction, and furthermore serves as a related chemical equipment supplier. Headquartered in Shanghai, Shanghai Hawkway Process Solutions & Equipment Co., Ltd. has an engineering design and inovation center. Jiangsu Hawkway Machinery Equipment Co., Ltd, located in Qidong of Jiangsu, mainly focusing on the manufacturing of the structure packing including metal sheet packings and wired mesh packing, trays and distributors, crystallizer, extraction column and polymer devolatilization and reaction equipment, all of which are of high quality at a competitive price.

The company is able to develop processes involving distillation, film evaporation, crystallization, mixing, polymerization, polymer devolatilization, modular facilities and etc. The services are exposed to a variety of fields, among which are petroleum refining, petrochemical, fine chemicals, grease chemicals, coal chemicals, steel chemicals, air separation, food, pharmaceutical and polymer industries. Of all the know-how and skills, the company has demonstrated unique expertise in the development of separation of isomers using a combination of distillation, crystallization and extraction columns, skid design and manufacturing, as well as polymer devolatilization and reaction.

Most of the team members have more than 15 years of working experiences in their respective departments such as process, engineering design and operational production. Since its establishment the company has supplied more than 100 process solutions of nearly 1,000 columns, with an annual sales turnover of about 0.1 billion RMB.
